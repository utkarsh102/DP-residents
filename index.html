Living Together

Society Rules & Guidelines

These guidelines keep Divyansh Pratham a great place for all 207 families. Please read and follow.

🔇

Noise & Disturbance

  • • Silence hours: 11:00 PM – 6:00 AM (strictly enforced)
  • • No loud music, DJ, or speaker systems after 10 PM
  • • Drilling and construction work only between 9 AM – 6 PM, Mon–Sat
  • • No honking inside the society premises
🅿️

Parking & Vehicles

  • • Park only in your allotted slot — violators will be towed
  • • No parking in fire lanes, near lifts, or visitor spots without a pass
  • • Visitor parking: maximum 48 hours; longer stays need office approval
  • • Commercial vehicles not permitted inside the society
  • • EV charging available at B4–B6 basement slots (₹8/unit)
🗑️

Waste & Cleanliness

  • • Segregate waste: Green = wet, Blue = dry, Purple = e-waste
  • • Do not throw garbage from windows or balconies — ₹2,000 fine
  • • Bulky items (furniture, appliances): inform office 24h before disposal
  • • Construction debris: resident's responsibility to arrange for removal
  • • No littering in common areas, stairwells, or lifts
🛠️

Renovation & Modifications

  • • Written approval required before any structural renovation
  • • Exterior modifications (grills, AC units, satellite dishes) need committee approval
  • • No changes to common walls, plumbing shafts, or electrical mains
  • • Renovation workers must deposit ID at the gate; access 9 AM – 6 PM only
  • • Security deposit of ₹10,000 required — refunded after inspection
🎊

Events & Celebrations

  • • Celebrations in common areas require advance booking at the office
  • • Flat celebrations must end by 11 PM; guests must exit by 11:30 PM
  • • Bursting of firecrackers is not permitted inside the society at any time
  • • DJs and live bands require special written permission from the President
  • • Guests using elevators after 10 PM must be accompanied by a resident
🔐

Security & Gate Policy

  • • All visitors must register at the gate with photo ID before entry
  • • Delivery agents allowed only up to lobby — not to individual floors
  • • Domestic helpers must be registered with the office (annual renewal)
  • • Do not share gate access codes with outsiders
  • • Report any suspicious activity immediately to security (Ext. 100)
💵

Maintenance & Fines

  • • Maintenance due by the 10th of each month; ₹500 fine after 25th
  • • Persistent defaulters (3+ months): facility access suspended
  • • Fines for rule violations are deducted from security deposit or charged separately
  • • Appeal process: written letter to Secretary within 7 days of fine notice

Full society bylaws available at the management office · Ground floor, Tower A · Office hours: Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 6 PM

Our Story

Ten years, 207 families, one address.

Divyansh Pratham was built with a single purpose: to give families in Indirapuram not just a place to live, but a place to belong. In the decade since our first families moved in, we've grown from neighbours into a genuine community.

We've celebrated Diwali and Holi together, watched the children of our early residents now going to college, and built shared spaces that families actually use — every single day.

The society is managed transparently by an elected committee. All accounts are shared at monthly General Body Meetings — because this is your home, and you deserve to know how it is run.
